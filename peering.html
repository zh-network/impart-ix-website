IIX Connecting Policy

Impart Internet Exchange (IIX)

- Peering Policy -

- Make Sure You Have -

-Have a valid public ASN
-Have up-to-date PeeringDB information (preferably)
-Have up-to-date RIR Whois information
-Only send traffic to destinations advertised by BGP on the Peering LAN
-Does not send any multicast (except ARP/IPv6 ND) packets
-Will not advertise the IIX addressing space outside of the IIX infrastructure.
-Will not abuse the Peering LAN for commercial services.
-Will not tunnel out the peering port or peering lan.
-MTU on the client interface must be at least 1500.

- Connection Rules -

Only allowed protocols (IPv4, IPv6, ARP) are used and protocols like Proxy ARP, OSPF, DHCP, STP/RSTP/VSTP with any kind of CDP/ZEROTIER are not allowed on peering lan

MAC-addresses are filtered per port

Routes learnt through peering network are not allowed to be announced by BGP

Connecting via any type of tunnel or VPN/Defragmented MTU is strictly prohibited.

If you are ready to connect, please enter this form to submit your request.
