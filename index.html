- About -

Welcome to Impart Internet Exchange (IIX), your gateway to seamless internet connectivity and expansion in Europe. At IIX, we are committed to providing you with unparalleled networking opportunities and access to a robust ecosystem of global partners.

With our state-of-the-art facility, you have the flexibility to choose from multiple connectivity options. You can physically connect within the renowned infrastructure of Hetzner Online, or seamlessly access other prominent internet exchanges such as DE-CIX and NL-IX through our transparent peering services. Additionally, we offer the convenience of purchasing connectivity through our trusted network of partner agents.

By joining IIX, you gain a competitive edge in the digital landscape. Expand your network reach, improve performance, and enhance user experience by leveraging our diverse peering opportunities. Our reliable infrastructure and strategic partnerships empower you to stay ahead of the curve, enabling seamless data exchange and fostering collaboration with industry leaders.
